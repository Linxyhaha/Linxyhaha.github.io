Xinyu Lin is a 3rd-year Ph.D student, supervised by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua in National University of Singapore. She obtained my M.Sc. degree from NUS in 2023, also supervised by Prof. Chua. Prior to that, She received B.Eng. degree from Shandong University in 2021. She is awarded the Google PhD Fellowship 2025. Xinyu's research interests include information retrieval, recommender system, and the intersection of LLMs with recommender system.
